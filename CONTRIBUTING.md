## üö¶ Rules of Engagement

### 1. Opening Issues
Please use Issues to:
* **Propose a Feature:** Suggest a new field for the Policy Bundle (e.g., "Geographical Execution Bounds").
* **Report a Concept Gap:** Identify a scenario where the current 3-layer integrity model might face challenges.
* **Ask a Question:** Clarify the mechanics of PSH canonicalization or Merkle selective disclosure.

### 2. The "No Unsolicited PRs" Policy
Because the core engine is proprietary and under development, **please do not submit Pull Requests containing code.** * PRs for documentation improvements or schema definitions (JSON/YAML) are welcome. 
* Unsolicited code PRs will be closed without review to protect our development roadmap and IP boundaries.

### 3. Professional Conduct
As a project focused on "Legitimacy and Identity," we expect all contributors to maintain a high level of professional discourse. Focus on technical merits, security proofs, and architectural integrity.
